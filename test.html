Hot Entree

We are constantly developing new hot entree items for our menus and use all natural ingredients. All meals are cooked fresh on-site and bake instead of fry.

Salad Bar

We offer a varied salad bar featuring 18 different toppings for students and teachers to choose from. The salad bar is one of our most popular options amongst our students.

Sandwhich

Sandwiches are a great option for students who don't like the hot entree for the day and do not wish to have a salad. Students can choose from a variety of breads, meats, and toppings.
